How Try Ons Work

On Curtsy you can try on any item for free. Because why should you have to pay for something that doesn’t fit?


Request up to 5 items

Pick out a backup and you’ll be sure to find something that works.

Meet up with the owner

Message and then meet up with the owner in a safe place that you decide together.

Take it home (optional)

If the owner allows it, you can try on items at home for 24 hours — great for figuring out shoes, approval from roommates and Facetiming mom.

Safe and easy payment

After trying on the item, Pay & Reserve the item through the app.

Heads up

The item isn't reserved until you pay. Other renters will be able to request it in the meantime.
