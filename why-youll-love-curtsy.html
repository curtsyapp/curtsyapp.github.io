Basics

It’s all renting

There’s no buying here. On Curtsy, you can rent fashion for a fraction of retail. All prices cover 3-7 day rentals.

It’s all local

On Curtsy, you rent from your neighbors. There’s no need to ship — and who likes shipping anyways?

Try on anything for free

Try on up to three items at once before you rent, so you’re sure to find something that works. Take up to two days.
