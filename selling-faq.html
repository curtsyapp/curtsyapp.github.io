For Sellers

  1. To list your first item, get a picture of the item, describe it, and set your price.
  2. When your item is purchased, Curtsy will send you a prepaid shipping label.
  3. You’ll be paid when the buyer receives your item.

For Buyers

  1. Browse fashion pieces including, dresses, skirts, rompers, sunglasses, shoes, & jewelry.
  2. Tap the “Buy Now” button to purchase. We’ll notify you with a tracking number when it ships!
  3. Rate the transaction. If anything is wrong message us in the app within 3 days of receiving your item.

How much should I sell my item for?

Just start a post and we’ll suggest price for your item, but you can price your items however you like.

What can I post on Curtsy

You can post Dresses, Rompers, Jumpsuits, Sets, Tops, Shorts, Skirts, Pants, & Activewear. If you would like to post other items, we'd love to hear from you.

Someone just bought my item, how/when am I paid?

You get paid as soon as your item ships. To get your shipping label, go to your orders in your profile. Print the label and take it with you to the post office with some tape and your good to go!

How to Ship

Curtsy provides you with USPS labels for your orders to make shiping simple and easy. Here is how it works:

  1. Print your shipping label
  2. Head to the post office with some tape
  3. Look for the shipping supplies and find the USPS Priority mail Padded Envelope. They are free.
  4. Seal the item in the Padded Envelope and drop it in the mail slot.
When using a label you don't have to wait in line -- just attach the labels and drop it off at the desk. You can even take some padded envelopes home with you. Then you can pack your item at home and drop it in any USPS blue bin.

What if my item doesn't fit?

Curtsy does not accept refunds but we do encourage you to relist the item if it does not work for you. When you relist, make sure to include accurate sizing details to ensure fit for the next buyer.

If the item was different than the description listed, please reach out to us.

When will I receive my order?

Most orders ship in 1-3 business days. We will notify you as soon as your item ships. Contact us for expedited shipping options or if you need a item by a specific date.

What are the fees of selling on Curtsy?

You keep 80% of your sale, and Curtsy’s commission is 20%.
