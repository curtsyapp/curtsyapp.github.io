You have two options when you rent on Curtsy



Try On Free

Tap “Try On” if you’re interested in renting the item but aren’t sure if it’ll work yet.

You’ll be asked to select the dates you’re interested in, and add a message to the owner.

The owner will respond and you will meet up with them to try on the item.

If it works, you’ll Pay & Reserve the item through Curtsy.

The item isn’t “reserved” until it’s paid for — until then, other renters will be able to reserve the item for the dates you need.

Reserve

Tap “Reserve” when you’re ready to rent and you don’t need to try on the item.

Your card will be charged and the owner will be paid instantly.
