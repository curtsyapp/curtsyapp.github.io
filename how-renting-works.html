You have two options when you rent on Curtsy

Shipping

Browse 70K+ items available for 2-3 day priority shipping to anywhere in the US

When you're ready to rent, press the "Request to Rent" button to set your rental dates and message the owner.

After the owner approves the rental, she'll ship the item 2-3 days before your selected rental date. You pay for the item when it ships.

You'll get the item with a prepaid return shipping label and instructions on how to mail it back.

Don't hesitate to contact Curtsy with any questions or concerns you have about the rental process

Local Pickup

When you want to rent an item available for local pickup, you have the options to try it on for free, or reseve it instantly

Press the "Try On" or "Reserve Now" button, and then select your dates and message the owner.

If it's a Try On, you pay when you confirm that the item fits. Otherwise, you pay when then owner confirms the rental.

Communicate with the owner to coordinate a time and place where you can meet up and try the item on and rent if it fits.

When it's time to return the item, message the owner and coordinate a dropoff.
