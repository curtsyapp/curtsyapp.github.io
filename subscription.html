We're changing our pricing.


Our members uploaded 80,000 items to Curtsy last year. Renters saved over $1 million renting these items instead of buying them new.

This is incredible — but we're just getting started.

Starting today, we're adjusting our pricing so that we can continue to invest in Curtsy your school, market to new users, and launch new features.

Here's what's changing


  • We lowered all prices by 20-30%.
  • We removed all fees from Curtsy. Before today, we charged renters a $3-6 insurance fee on each rental, and charged owners 30%.
  • Owners now keep 100% of their earnings.


Membership


Instead of charging fees on rentals, we’re launching a new membership.

You'll need a membership to rent and browse items in the app.

You don’t need a membership to upload and rent out your closet as an owner.

When owners make more, more people upload their closet, leading to more options for renters — everybody wins.

Existing users (that's you!) who sign up this week will get two weeks free when they join.
